The Podcast: Queer Kahani

A quirky, queer podcast of bite-sized stories - happy stories, sad stories, first loves, worst loves, epiphanies, recipes, and everything in between. Episodes are available here and coming soon to Spotify and Apple Music. Story submissions and feedback are welcome by email.

The Book: A Boy Named Su

A genderqueer journey through a gender-binary world, A Boy Named Su traces the footsteps of the author's confusion, shame, and eventual embrace of his sexuality and gender identity. Now available at Elliott Bay Books, Amazon, Medium (free), and goodreads.
